## 🌟 General Questions

### What is Yaqeen?

Yaqeen is a comprehensive Islamic digital platform that provides:
- **Quran Studies**: Complete Quran text with translations and tafsir
- **Hadith Collections**: Authentic hadith from established sources
- **Educational Tools**: Learning resources and interactive features
- **Spiritual Content**: Adhkar, duas, and Islamic articles

Our mission is to make Islamic knowledge accessible through modern technology while maintaining authenticity and scholarly accuracy.

### Is Yaqeen free to use?

Yes! Yaqeen is completely free and open-source. We believe Islamic knowledge should be accessible to everyone without barriers.

### What languages does Yaqeen support?

Currently, Yaqeen primarily supports Arabic with English translations. We plan to add more languages including:
- Urdu
- Indonesian
- French
- German
- And more based on community demand

### How accurate is the content?

We prioritize accuracy and authenticity:
- **Quran Text**: Sourced from authenticated manuscripts
- **Translations**: From reputable scholars and institutions
- **Hadith**: From sahih (authentic) collections with grading
- **Tafsir**: From established classical and contemporary sources

All content is reviewed by Islamic scholars and subject matter experts.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Why is the page not loading?

Common solutions:
- **Check your internet connection**
- **Clear browser cache** (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
- **Try a different browser**
- **Disable browser extensions** temporarily
- **Check our status page** for outages

### Why are some features not working?

Possible causes:
- **Browser compatibility** - Try Chrome or Firefox
- **JavaScript disabled** - Enable JavaScript in browser settings
- **Ad blockers** - Some features may be blocked
- **Outdated browser** - Update to the latest version

### How do I clear my browser cache?

**Chrome:**
1. Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
2. Select "Cached images and files"
3. Click "Clear data"

**Firefox:**
1. Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
2. Select "Cache"
3. Click "Clear"

### The audio is not playing. What should I do?

Audio issues can be caused by:
- **Browser restrictions** - Click anywhere on the page first
- **Ad blockers** - Disable for Yaqeen
- **Network issues** - Check your internet connection
- **Browser settings** - Ensure media autoplay is enabled

### I'm having trouble with Arabic text display.

Arabic text issues:
- **Install Arabic fonts** on your system
- **Enable RTL support** in browser settings
- **Use a compatible browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
- **Check font rendering** settings
