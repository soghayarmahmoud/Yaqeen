# 🗺️ Roadmap

This document outlines the planned development roadmap for **Yaqeen**. It's a living document that evolves with our community and project needs.

## 🎯 Vision

Yaqeen aims to be the most comprehensive and user-friendly Islamic digital platform, bridging ancient Islamic wisdom with modern technology to serve millions of users worldwide.

## 📅 Current Status

**Version**: 0.1.0 (Alpha)
**Status**: Active Development
**Next Milestone**: Beta Release (v1.0.0)
